Email Alerts

Optionally configure email notifications for sync failures and issues.

Email alerts notify you about sync failures, repeated errors, and other issues that need attention. This is optional — you can also monitor sync status from the dashboard. You can configure this later in Admin Settings.

587 for STARTTLS, 465 for SSL

For Gmail: use an App Password (requires 2-factor authentication on your Google account).

Comma-separated list of email addresses to receive alert notifications
